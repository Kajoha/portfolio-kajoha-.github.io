I more creative

Creativity is divergence of thoughts, where we explore different concepts to create unique, authentic things, however, in the world of design CREATIVITY IS NOT EVERYTHING, the creative process not enough, must also be scientific, since design must be tested, and must be intuitive, every decision about a design has to be justified, when these two concepts converge, I explore my most creative self.

Projects

Palabras Mayores was a design project carried out for the journalist Silvia Zeledón, which tries to expose how older adults are still an economic force for the country, for this project I was responsible for the communication of the brand, the wireframes of the website and also the digital marketing strategy.

COLECTIVE DESIGN One of the most important collective design projects in which I participated was the realization of a mural to commemorate the week of women, it was an important experience for me since the participants were refugee women, getting to agree that designing was quite a process, we explore talks, group activities.

During these last 3 years I have worked on many design projects, be it brand, communicational strategies or artistic explorations, all of them have helped me develop a critical vision about how users perceive information.
